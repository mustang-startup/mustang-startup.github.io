Welcome to Toronto!

Visiting for the FIFA World Cup? Discover the best of Toronto—dining, entertainment, and must-see attractions—all in one place. Enjoy your stay and make the most of your World Cup experience!

Top Dining Spots

St. Lawrence Market

Iconic food market with local and international flavors. Try the famous peameal bacon sandwich!

Kensington Market

Eclectic neighborhood with global eats, vegan options, and vibrant street art.

Chinatown

Authentic Asian cuisine, from dim sum to pho, in a bustling district near downtown.

Entertainment & Nightlife

Distillery District

Historic area with bars, galleries, live music, and unique shops.

Queen Street West

Trendy strip for nightlife, indie music venues, and boutique shopping.

Harbourfront Centre

Waterfront events, outdoor concerts, and beautiful lake views.

Must-See Attractions

CN Tower

Toronto’s iconic skyline landmark. Go up for breathtaking city views!

Royal Ontario Museum

World-class exhibits on art, culture, and natural history.

Toronto Islands

Take a ferry for beaches, biking, and a relaxing escape from downtown.
